Security Awareness

Scammers may contact you through emails, messages, calls, links, or files.

They use pressure and urgency — don’t act quickly.

  • Verify unexpected requests.
  • Do not share passwords or codes.
  • Be careful with links and attachments.
  • If something feels off, stop and check.
